The Journey Behind the Creator Hub

Hello! I'm the face behind this channel and community. I'm a **45-year-old vlogger** and **global traveler**, and my story is one of movement, change, and finding a new purpose through exploration.

From Iran to the World

I was born in **Iran**, and my early life was defined by migration. My family first moved to **Denmark**, where I spent my formative years, before we finally settled and made our home here in the **United States**. These different cultures shaped who I am today—a storyteller with a unique perspective on life, culture, and what it means to belong.

The Unexpected Start to Vlogging

I always dreamed of sharing my travels and stories, but the timing never seemed right. That changed recently, and I finally launched my **YouTube channel at the beginning of 2025**.

The success I’ve found was completely unexpected. On New Year's Day 2025, I recorded a short, simple video of me **entering the NYC subway**, and it completely **went viral**. It was the push I needed, proving there was an audience out there eager to join my journey.

Finding Strength Through Travel

The decision to start traveling the world intensively and begin vlogging was deeply personal. Last year, in **September 2024**, I lost my **father to lung cancer**. It was an incredibly difficult and painful period. Travel became my **coping mechanism**—a way to process grief, find beauty in the world, and turn my energy outward into creation.

Now, every video I make and every place I visit is part of that journey, honoring his memory by living life fully. I'm sharing not just the incredible destinations, but the honest, day-to-day experiences of a traveler and vlogger navigating the world.
